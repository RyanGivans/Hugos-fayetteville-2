The Story

A Fayetteville favorite that still feels like itself.

Hugo's has been in business since 1977, located in the basement at 25 1/2 N Block Ave off the Historic Fayetteville Square. The menu remains true to its roots: burgers, eclectic appetizers, entrees, desserts, homemade french fries, beer cheese soup, and grasshopper crepes.

The bar brings rotating seasonal and craft beer draughts, a dedicated local tap, and an enviable whiskey, scotch, and bourbon collection.
